Chore Dash

Winner of ACC Game Dev 2023

Chore Dash is a game developed in python and it is based around a character who has to finish their chores before their mom gets home. There are numerous activities the player can do including a minigame where the player has to battle dirty dishes. The game was developed by a team of 4 and we were given a week to complete the game.

The Movie List

Movie Ranking App

The Movie List is a movie ranking app that allows users to create an account and rank movies. The app was developed using the MERN stack, MongoDB, Express, React, and Node. The app is still in development and more features are being added.
